About Me

Dr. Narahari

Welcome to my educational platform! I am a passionate aerospace educator with extensive experience in both academic and industry settings. My mission is to make complex aerospace concepts accessible to students worldwide.

  • Published researcher with 50+ peer-reviewed papers
